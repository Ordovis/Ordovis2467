What is Riot Games ?

  • Riot Games is a video game developer and publisher known for its flagship title, League of Legends, one of the most popular and influential online multiplayer battle arena (MOBA) games globally. The company was founded in 2006 and has since expanded its offerings, including the development of new games and esports initiatives, making it a significant player in the gaming industry. Riot Games is committed to creating immersive gaming experiences and fostering competitive gaming communities through various titles and projects.
  • We are most prominently recognized for its ownership of the immensely popular and highly competitive game, League of Legends. Additionally, Riot Games has expanded its gaming portfolio with titles like Valorant, a tactical first-person shooter, and Teamfight Tactics, a strategic auto-battler, both of which have garnered dedicated player bases. With a commitment to delivering high-quality gaming experiences, Riot Games continues to explore new ventures in the world of video game development and esports, solidifying its position as a significant player in the industry.
