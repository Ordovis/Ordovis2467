Why Riot Games?

Key Reasons Riot Games Was Founded:

  • Passion for Games: The founders of Riot Games were passionate gamers themselves and wanted to create the kind of games they loved to play.
  • Player Empowerment: Riot Games wanted to give players a voice and make them an integral part of the game development process.
  • eSports Revolution: Riot Games revolutionized eSports with their flagship title, League of Legends, making it a global competitive phenomenon.
  • Innovation: Riot Games sought to innovate within the gaming industry, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in online gaming.
  • Community Building: Building a strong and positive gaming community was a central goal of Riot Games, fostering connections between players worldwide.

Our Mission

Riot Games is dedicated to developing high-quality, immersive, and competitive games that bring joy and excitement to millions of players worldwide.
Our mission is to continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the gaming world while keeping the player experience at the heart of everything we do.
